C H A N G I N G  T H E  V I E W

Loq Airou lets you control many aspects of the view using the mouse. For this to be efficient, athree-button mouse is highly recommended (the scroll wheel on typical mice is referred to as the middle button, below).

The mouse bindings used for controlling the view are:

  • By pressing the right mouse button you can rotate around the model.
  • By pressing the left button while holding the right down you can zoom in and out.
  • By pressing the left button while holding the right down you can pan the view.
  • If you press both the left and middle buttons while holding down the right one, you can reset the view.
  • Right-clicking on the manipulator focuses the view around the manipulator.
Learning these is essential for getting the most out of Loq Airou.
