H U L L  R E S H A P E

This tool allows you to reshape a continuous line of edges and is very useful for sculpting the shape of an object. Start by left clicking on an edge between two selected vertexes. The edge and edges connected will now be hi-lighted and you can drag in the direction of the edge. When you pass the vertexes in the junctions between the edges the vertices will move to the position of the pointer. Tool works on both directions so you can at any time switch direction, this gives you the opportunity to re-draw an edge again and again until the desired shape is produced.
